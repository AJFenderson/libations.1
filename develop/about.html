Our Story

Who doesn't like a good bar-hop night with friends? Though our founders know their local hot spots, whenever they traveled outside of their city, they had a hard time finding that place that only locals know is special. The problem was that, by the time they find a local whose enthusiasm for a good time matched theirs, the reliability of information would diminish as the night waned. Founded in 2020 by Annaruth McBride and Cha Alexander, Libations was created to solve that specific problem.
